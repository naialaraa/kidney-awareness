How to Take Care of Your Kidneys

  • Stay Hydrated: Drink plenty of water to help your kidneys flush out toxins.
  • Eat a Balanced Diet: Focus on fruits, vegetables, and whole grains while limiting salt and processed foods.
  • Exercise Regularly: Maintain a healthy weight and improve overall kidney function.
  • Avoid Smoking and Alcohol: These can damage kidney tissues over time.
  • Monitor Blood Pressure and Blood Sugar: Keep them within a healthy range.
  • Limit Over-the-Counter Painkillers: Excessive use of NSAIDs can harm your kidneys.
  • Get Regular Check-ups: Especially if you have risk factors like diabetes or high blood pressure.
